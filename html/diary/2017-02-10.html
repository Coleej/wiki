Summary

KX wanted QC to know about the instrument deployment data, Feb. 25th, in case QC wanted to include any instruments. Ideas were pressure transducers, wave gauges, and ADVs. Hobos on the land surface to get an idea about inundation and wave gauges on the sub-aqueous perimeter of the "crab claw" for water level boundary condition.

The plans for field work were discussed. The dates for building a platform are Feb. 17-19 and initial deployment on Feb. 25. The idea is now to model the "crab claw" because RT and DE think that an integrated carbon budget over the entire island is necessary to publish and KX can't logistically accomplish this with data collection.

For field work, there needs to be pressure transducers on the marsh surface to measure water level and the ISCO/ADV to measure carbon flux. RT and KX have the details on this.
